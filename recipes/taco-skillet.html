Taco skillet

Description

Easy taco skillet bake that uses only one pan. This recipe can be made for under $10!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Water
  • Mexican cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir beef and onion until beef is browned
  2. Stir tomatoes, water, rice, and taco seasoning into beef mixture and bring to a boil.
  3. Top with Mexican cheese blend and lettuce before serving.
